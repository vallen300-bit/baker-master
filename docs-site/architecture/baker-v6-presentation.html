Who decides what

The bank model

You authorise; the fleet executes. Decisions fall into three tiers — only one of them ever reaches your desk.

⬢ Director
Final say — capital, counterparties, anything externally-facing
◆ AH1
Orchestrates the fleet; runs everything inside the green lane
⚙ 🗂 🧭 Build · desks · universal
Feed AH1 — build-pool agents never reach the Director directly
Tier A — automatic

AH1 just does it: dispatch, merge on green review, routine commits. You hear about it after.

Tier B — ask first

AH1 prepares the exact action and asks "Shall I?" — env changes, external posts, secrets.

Tier C — you only

Money, counterparty drafts, speaking as the Chairman. The fleet surfaces; never acts.
