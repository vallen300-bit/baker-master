The Opportunity

European luxury hotel development is a EUR 50 billion annual market with essentially zero AI penetration. The developers who build the right AI first will own a category that does not yet exist.

Luxury hotel development remains untouched by AI for structural reasons: proprietary data is locked inside family-owned groups, the domain is too complex for generic tools, and decision-makers trust only people who have built what they are building. This creates a rare window for an insider to build — and own — the solution.

Corinthia is uniquely positioned to seize this. Through QP and Disigneq, the Group already controls the full vertical: design, engineering, project management, and operations. Adding an AI intelligence layer transforms this vertical from analogue to intelligent — and creates a new revenue line that can be offered to third-party clients.

Brisen AI — Proven, Not Theoretical

Brisen Group built four AI products out of necessity — as an owner-developer on a EUR 250M Mandarin Oriental project, not as a technology company. The products work because they were forged in real construction:

ClaimsMax
Ingests 50,000+ pages of construction documentation. Cross-references contracts, variations, and invoices. Identifies recoverable claims in 30 days vs. 6-12 months manually.
TenderMax
Translates 1,500-page brand standards into procurement specifications. Generates tender packages, evaluates bids, optimises supplier selection across concurrent projects.
OwnerView
Portfolio-wide intelligence dashboard. Benchmarks cost-per-key, programme compliance, and contractor performance across geographies and currencies. Real-time, not quarterly.
SiteWatch
Computer vision for construction sites. Compares observed progress against planned schedule from continuous camera feeds. Objective verification replacing contractor self-reporting.

Why Corinthia + Brisen AI

Corinthia brings what no technology company can replicate: 60 years of proprietary development data across 17+ properties, a vertically integrated development capability (QP + Disigneq), and a pipeline spanning Rome to Riyadh to Dubai. Brisen brings deployed, revenue-generating AI products proven on a comparable ultra-luxury project.

The key insight: Corinthia's data makes the AI smarter, but the data never leaves Corinthia's control. The AI comes to QP's data — not the other way around. Corinthia gains a proprietary intelligence advantage that no competitor can replicate, because no competitor has Corinthia's data.
