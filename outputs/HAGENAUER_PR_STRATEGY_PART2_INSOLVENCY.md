## 1. NARRATIVE SHIFT -- THE INSOLVENCY CHANGES EVERYTHING

### 1.1 What Happened

On 27 March 2026, Hagenauer Austria filed full insolvency for both the GmbH and the GmbH & Co KG. The Schutzschirmverfahren (protective shield proceedings) that began on approximately 16 March failed after only eleven days. An Insolvenzverwalter (insolvency administrator) will be appointed by the court. All pending litigation is stayed under Section 7 of the Insolvenzordnung (IO).

Separately and critically: Christine Saehn (Hagenauer project management) sent a letter to all subcontractors -- dozens of companies -- naming Dimitry Vallen and Edita Vallen personally as "faktische Geschaeftsfuehrer" (de facto managing directors) and attributing the insolvency to their conduct. This letter constitutes a deliberate escalation and is potentially actionable under Section 1330 ABGB (Kreditschaedigung / credit defamation).

### 1.2 Why This Changes the Narrative

The story has fundamentally shifted. Before the insolvency:

- **Old narrative:** "Developer refuses to pay contractor EUR 15 million"
- **New narrative:** "Contractor who accused developer of non-payment was itself insolvent"

This is the single most important reframing opportunity in the entire dispute. The insolvency does the following for Brisen's position:

1. **Validates Brisen's concerns.** Brisen questioned Hagenauer's financial capacity. Hagenauer and his lawyers called this unfounded. The insolvency proves Brisen was right.

2. **Destroys the EUR 15M claim credibility.** A company that files for insolvency eleven days after commencing protective shield proceedings was not "owed EUR 15 million" by one client. It was structurally insolvent across its entire business. The self-reduction from EUR 13.2M to EUR 5.85M -- already a devastating admission -- now reads as a desperate attempt to extract cash before the inevitable filing.

3. **Exposes the media campaign as a shakedown.** The litigation PR campaign orchestrated by Nemetschke/NHK -- the articles planted in ImmoFokus, the letter to Mandarin Oriental London, the subcontractor outreach -- was not a legitimate effort to collect a valid debt. It was a pre-insolvency pressure campaign designed to extract payment before the business collapsed.

4. **Shifts sympathy to Brisen on subcontractors.** Hagenauer received EUR 130M+ from Brisen but failed to pay his own supply chain. The subcontractors are Hagenauer's victims, not Brisen's.

5. **Makes the "faktischer Geschaeftsfuehrer" allegation look desperate.** Blaming the client for your own insolvency -- in a letter sent to dozens of companies on the day you file -- is not a credible legal position. It is a scorched-earth tactic by a failing management team.

### 1.3 How to Exploit This

Sandra should reframe every future communication through this lens. The core pivot is:

> **From defensive** ("we paid 95%, here is our side of the story") **to authoritative** ("the insolvency confirms what we knew -- the contractor was in financial distress, and Brisen is now protecting the project and the subcontractors").

This is not about gloating. The tone must remain measured. But the posture changes from "responding to allegations" to "managing a situation we anticipated."

## 5. THE "FAKTISCHER GESCHAEFTSFUEHRER" PR ANGLE

### 5.1 Why the PR Response Must Differ from the Legal Response

E+H is handling the legal dimension: Section 1330 ABGB injunction assessment, personal counsel engagement for Dimitry and Edita, analysis of the Punkt 26 TUV clause, and potential Forderungsanmeldung. This is technical, slow, and confidential.

The PR dimension is different. The Saehn letter has been sent to dozens of companies. The allegation is now circulating in the construction industry supply chain. Every subcontractor, every trade foreman, every project manager in Vienna's luxury construction sector has received a letter naming the Vallens as the people who "caused" Hagenauer's bankruptcy. This is a reputational poisoning that operates independently of the legal process.

### 5.2 The PR Neutralization Strategy

**Step 1: Name it for what it is (this week)**

In all communications -- press responses, stakeholder briefings, subcontractor outreach -- the Saehn letter should be characterized as:

> "A baseless allegation circulated on the day of the insolvency filing, naming the shareholders of the client company as responsible for the contractor's own financial failure. We note that the contractor was simultaneously in restructuring proceedings in Germany, had lost its co-founder three weeks earlier, and had seen its own claims reduced by more than half upon examination. The timing and content of this letter are being reviewed by our legal counsel."

This framing achieves three things:
1. It establishes the allegation as baseless without engaging in the legal specifics
2. It connects the Saehn letter to the insolvency timeline -- making it look like a last-ditch blame-shift
3. It signals legal consequences without specifying them

**Step 2: Inoculate key relationships (this week)**

Sandra should prepare a one-paragraph briefing statement that Thomas Leitner or Dennis Egorenkov can use in direct conversations with:
- MOHG contacts (Eva Tzivanaki-Morris, Mario Habicher)
- Key subcontractors Brisen wants to retain
- Banks/lenders
- Aukera/Wilmington

The paragraph:

> "You may be aware that a letter was circulated by Hagenauer's former project manager on the day of the insolvency filing, making certain allegations about the Vallen family. These allegations are factually false and legally unfounded. We are taking appropriate steps. We wanted to make you aware directly so that you have the correct information."

**Step 3: Do NOT issue a public rebuttal of the Saehn letter (yet)**

The Saehn letter is a private communication to subcontractors. It has not (yet) been published by media. If Brisen issues a public rebuttal, it risks amplifying the allegation to a wider audience. The response should remain targeted: legal action via E+H, direct briefing of key stakeholders, and readiness to respond if media picks it up.

If ImmoFokus or another outlet publishes the "faktischer Geschaeftsfuehrer" allegation, deploy Q4 from the Q&A above immediately.
