# 4. USE CASES: WHERE THE TRIANGLE CREATES TANGIBLE VALUE

## Use Case 1: Corinthia Rome -- Post-Completion Claims Recovery

**The scenario:** Corinthia Rome (opened March 2026, 60 rooms) has just completed construction. Heritage building in central Rome. Italian regulatory environment. Complex contractor landscape.

**The triangle at work:**
- **Corinthia** provides: All construction documentation -- contracts, change orders, correspondence, invoices, snag lists, handover protocols
- **Brisen AI (ClaimsMax)** processes: 50,000+ pages of documentation ingested and cross-referenced. AI identifies discrepancies between contracted scope, delivered work, and final invoicing. Maps every contractor obligation against every deliverable.
- **NVIDIA** powers: DGX Cloud processes the document corpus. Large-context models (running on NVIDIA H100/B200 GPUs) handle the 50,000-page analysis that would take a human claims team 6-12 months.

**The result:** EUR 2-5M in recoverable claims identified within 30 days. For comparison: ClaimsMax identified EUR 6M on MO Vienna (EUR 250M project). Corinthia Rome (smaller project, EUR ~100M estimated) could yield EUR 2-5M at similar extraction rates.

**Who benefits:** Corinthia recovers capital. Brisen demonstrates multi-project scalability. NVIDIA gets a case study in document AI for construction.

## Use Case 4: Construction Site Intelligence (SiteWatch)

**The scenario:** Corinthia Maldives (100 rooms, opening 2028) is under construction on a remote island. Site visits are expensive. Progress monitoring depends on manual reports from contractors who have incentives to overstate completion.

**The triangle at work:**
- **Corinthia** provides: Site access, camera installation permissions, construction programme/schedule
- **Brisen AI (SiteWatch)** processes: Computer vision analysis of continuous camera feeds. Compares observed progress against planned schedule. Identifies safety violations, material waste, and progress discrepancies in real time.
- **NVIDIA** powers: This is the most GPU-intensive product. Real-time video analysis across multiple camera feeds requires NVIDIA GPU acceleration (inference on edge devices + cloud processing). This is NVIDIA's "Physical AI" narrative in action.

**The result:** 24/7 automated construction monitoring. Real-time progress verification. Immediate alerts on safety or quality issues. Reduction in site visit costs by 50%+. Objective, AI-verified progress reporting that replaces contractor self-reporting.

**Who benefits:** Corinthia gets eyes on a remote site 24/7. Brisen deploys its most GPU-heavy product. NVIDIA gets a Physical AI showcase in construction -- a category Jensen Huang has explicitly prioritized.

**The result:** Data-driven pricing that maximizes revenue while maintaining absorption velocity.
