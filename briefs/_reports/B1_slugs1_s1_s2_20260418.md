### Recommendation: open SLUGS-2 as a design ticket before any migration

Neither fold-all-in-one NOR per-file migration is right *today*. Rationale in §5 of the catalogue:

**Not fold-all:** The 3 structural residuals share ONE root problem (vocabulary drift) but require THREE distinct schema decisions (YAML `paths:` field; `canonical_slug` FK vs YAML absorption; `_MATTER_PATTERNS` retirement timing). Folding without those decisions = either time-pressured schema choices or an incoherent PR.

**Not per-file now:** Two of three have better replacements coming. `_MATTER_PATTERNS` is replaced wholesale by KBL-B. `_seed_matter_registry` needs Director review on 5 non-canonical names (`FX Mayr`, `ClaimsMax`, `Lanas`, `Alric`, `NVIDIA-GTC-2026`) — promote to canonical or retire. No design consensus → no safe migration. Only `PATH_MATTER_HINTS` is cleanly standalone, but doing it in isolation leaves 2/3 of the drift.

**Proposed SLUGS-2 ticket shape** (from catalogue §5):

1. Design phase (Director + AI Head): schema calls for (a) YAML `paths:` field, (b) DB `canonical_slug` FK vs full absorption into YAML, (c) KBL-B timing for `_MATTER_PATTERNS` retirement.
2. Director labeling phase: fate of 5 non-canonical display names.
3. Implementation phase: fold-all-in-one PR, schema pinned first.

Est. 2-3 hr design + 4-6 hr implementation + tests. Not a 30-min task.
