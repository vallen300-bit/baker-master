# Future Baker Optimization — Parked Ideas

**Owner:** AI Head
**Purpose:** Park ideas, design sketches, and opportunistic improvements that are **out of Cortex T3 critical path** but worth revisiting once the pipeline is in production.

---

## Rules

1. **Nothing in this folder is scheduled.** Entries are parked for future consideration — Director ratifies when to unpark.
2. **Each parked item must include:**
   - `Parked date`
   - `Parked by` (AI Head, R agent, B3, Director, etc.)
   - `Reason parked` (one line — usually: "competes with T3 ship-first priority")
   - `Value estimate` (one line — why this matters)
   - `Rough cost` (one line — implementation time estimate)
   - `Dependencies` (what must ship or be decided first)
3. **On unpark:** AI Head moves the item from here into `briefs/_tasks/` or authors an active implementation brief, then removes the parked copy.
4. **Review cadence:** After each major Cortex milestone (T3 production, first Director-Gold-promotion cycle, first full week of Opus signals), AI Head re-scans this folder for items whose dependencies have landed.
