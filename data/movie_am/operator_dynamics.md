## Key Personnel Provisions (MA 3.3)

### GM Appointment
- Mandarin has **exclusive right** to hire, promote, discharge, supervise all Hotel staff
- **GM candidates:** Up to 3 presented sequentially; Owner interviews each. If Owner rejects 2, the 3rd does not require approval [MA 3.3.1]
- **FC, Dir Sales, Dir F&B:** Up to 2 candidates; if Owner rejects 1st, the 2nd does not require approval [MA 3.3.1]
- GM is always a **Mandarin Employee** (costs borne by Owner as operating expense) [MA 3.3.4]
- Mandarin must **consult Owner** before transferring GM [MA 3.3.3]

### Non-Solicitation (MA 3.3.6)
- Owner may not employ/solicit any person who was a Mandarin employee or MOHG staff at the Hotel in the preceding year

### Trade Unions (MA 3.3.5)
- Mandarin conducts negotiations but **with Owner's participation in all material respects**
- Neither party can sign without the other's approval

## Non-Interference Clause (MA 7.2 — exact wording)
Owner shall not: (a) interfere with Mandarin's rights to manage/operate; (b) enter contracts that materially affect operations; (c) issue instructions to Hotel staff or contractors.

**HOWEVER:** Owner may express views in meetings (MA 7.3), and Mandarin must give due regard to Owner's concerns. Owner may also engage asset managers, operating and technical professionals at own expense — Mandarin must cooperate and provide information. This does NOT constitute interference.

## Restricted Contracts (MA 3.7 — what MO cannot do without consent)
- Any lease/licence/concession with annual payments >EUR 100,000
- Any contract relating to Hotel with annual payments >EUR 100,000
- Any restaurant/fitness/spa management or licensing contract (ANY amount)
- Any outside consultant on substantial Hotel matters (ANY amount)
- **Excluded:** contracts expiring within 1 year, employment agreements, guest bookings, loyalty programmes

## Meeting Obligations (MA 7.3)

| Meeting | Frequency | Who Must Attend | Content |
|---------|-----------|----------------|---------|
| Senior MOHG executives | Quarterly (on Owner request) | Senior executives + Owner | Operations/performance review |
| GM + Director of Finance | Monthly (or agreed schedule) | GM, Finance Director, Owner | P&L presentation, Q&A on results vs budget, client relationship, competitor activity |

**Critical quote:** "Mandarin acknowledges that routine communications or consultation by Owner with the General Manager does not constitute 'interference' under Clause 7.2."

## Owner's Audit & Oversight Rights
- All accounting records kept at the Hotel, per IFRS + Uniform System 11th ed [CSA 6.1]
- Owner-authorized persons may examine and copy at all reasonable times with reasonable notice [CSA 6.1]
- Owner may engage asset managers, operating/technical experts at any time, at own expense [CSA 3.1]
- Annual audit by independent auditor selected by **Owner**, approved by Mandarin [CSA 6.3]
- Owner may conduct separate review/audit at own expense [CSA 6.3]
