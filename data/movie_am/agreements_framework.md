### Performance Test (MA 11.5) — CRITICAL
- Starts: **9th Fiscal Year**
- Test period: 2 consecutive FYs
- **RevPAR Test:** Hotel RevPAR must equal or exceed 3rd highest in Competitive Set
- **Threshold Test (alternative):** AGOP >= 90% of budgeted AGOP OR positive ANOI
- **Both tests must fail** in each of 2 consecutive FYs to trigger termination right
- Competitive Set: **Park Hyatt Vienna, Hotel Imperial, Hotel Bristol, Sacher Hotel** [reviewed every 3 years]
- Source: Smith Travel Research (STAR)
- Cure: Mandarin may pay Cure Amount within 90 days (max twice during Initial Period, once per extension)
- Under-Performance Notice: within 60 days of test results; termination effective 120+ days later

### Cross-Termination (MA 11.6)
- Termination of any Hotel Agreement triggers right to terminate all on same date

## Territorial Restriction (MA 3.8)
- No competing "Mandarin Oriental" luxury hotel in City of Vienna during Term
- **Chain Exception** (from 15th anniversary of Opening): 5+ hotel portfolio acquisitions exempt
- **Residences restriction:** until later of (i) 93% of Residences sold AND (ii) 10th anniversary of Opening Date

## Pre-Emptive Right (MA 14.3)
- During Initial Period, Owner must first offer to Mandarin on same terms as third party offer
- Mandarin has 30 days to accept, then 30 days to complete
- If declined, Owner can sell for not less than 95% of offered price within 1 year
