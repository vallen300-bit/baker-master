# AO Psychology Profile

## Core Archetype: The Hunter
Most alive during the chase, loses interest after the kill. Sausage-making (maintenance) bores him. Every interaction must be framed as a hunt, not housekeeping.

## Core Relationship
22-year relationship since 2004. Started with AO as LP in UK real estate development. Predates all current entities, advisors, and structures.

## Critical Vulnerability
> "You only call me when you need money." — AO

AO feels used as a cash source when contact is transactional. The relationship must be maintained BETWEEN capital events, not only during them. DV acknowledges he always forgets this.

**Baker Standing Order:** Baker MUST proactively prompt DV to reach out to AO during quiet periods — share updates, hotel wins, press coverage, deal pipeline news, personal check-ins. The WORST thing is silence followed by a capital call. The cadence of attention must be INDEPENDENT of the cadence of asks.

**Rule:** Never let more than 2 weeks pass without AO receiving something — an update, a win, a question asking his opinion, a photo from the hotel. If Baker detects a gap approaching 14 days with no AO-directed communication, flag it to DV immediately.

## Loyalty Algorithm (Ettore Case Study)

AO rewards personal investment in him, even without formal business. Decoded from his relationship with Ettore (rich Italian ~80yo, cement factory on US East Coast, uncle figure).

No business relationship with Ettore. But Ettore:
- (a) Connects AO to big flyers and interesting people
- (b) Always brings gifts — expensive wine, truffles, cheese
- (c) Helped AO buy a car at personal loss

AO values all three so much he said he will find a way to be grateful.

### Four Loyalty Triggers
1. **Access** — Connecting AO to interesting, powerful people (big flyers). Social capital > business deals.
2. **Gestures** — Never arriving empty-handed. Expensive wine, truffles, cheese. The cultural code of generosity matters deeply.
3. **Selfless Service** — Doing something for AO at personal cost. Ettore helped buy a car, lost money on it. AO values sacrifice.
4. **Reciprocity** — AO said he will find a way to be grateful to Ettore. Loyalty earns future reward — undefined but certain.
